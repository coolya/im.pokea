Never miss any Android Notification
while working on your Mac.

simple

Simply install Pokea on your Mac and Android, connect and magically see your notifications appear on the Mac.

energy saving

Pokea is using the newest Bluetooth 4.0 LE technology so you can use it all day without affecting your battery life.

private

Your notification never leave your devices. Since Pokea is using Bluetooth no third party or cloud service is involved. Nobody else can read your notifications.

Requirements

You need Mavericks on your Mac to use Pokea. Also your Android Phone and Mac have to be Bluetooth 4.0 LE enabled. Your Phone has to run at least Android 4.3. For compatible Macs see below.

iMac

2012 or newer

Mac mini

2011 or newer

Mac Pro

2013 or newer

MacBook Air

2011 or newer
